Tunis is the sprawling capital of Tunisia, a country in North Africa.
It sits along Lake Tunis, just inland from the Mediterranean Sea’s Gulf of Tunis.
It’s home to a centuries-old medina and the Bardo,
an archaeology museum where celebrated Roman mosaics are displayed in a 15th-century palace complex.
The parklike ruins of ancient Carthage sit in the city’s northern suburbs.
