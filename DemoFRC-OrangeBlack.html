Tunis was founded by the Libyans, who in the 9th century BCE surrendered the site of Carthage to the Phoenicians from Tyre.
In 146 BCE, during the Third Punic War between Carthage and Rome, Tunis and Carthage were destroyed.
The city flourished under Roman rule,but its importance dates chiefly from the Muslim conquest in the 7th century CE.
